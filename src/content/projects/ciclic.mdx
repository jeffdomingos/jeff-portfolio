        ## Challenge

        Ciclic launched a new residential assistance product, a 100% online

        ## My role

        As a Service and UX Designer (freelancer) for Estúdio Marte, I
        collaborated with the team and Ciclic stakeholders to identify user pain
        points and improve the product's user experience and marketability.
    - type: image

    - type: text
      content: >-
        ## Design process

        We employed a Design Sprint 2.0 framework, conducting in-depth user
        research through interviews and surveys. This research was enriched with
        three key workshops:


        *   **Proto-Persona Development:** We created user profiles to
        understand target audience needs and expectations.

        *   **User Journey Mapping:** We visualized the user's experience from
        initial awareness to service completion.

        *   **Co-creation of Ideas:** Brainstorming with the team and
        stakeholders generated solutions for product improvement and
        communication.
    - type: image
      src: /images/projects/ciclic/02.gif
      alt: The design thinking behind the design building
    - type: text
      content: >-
        ## Impact

        We developed and tested five high-fidelity prototypes for the product's
        online sales pages (mobile and desktop), evaluating aspects like
        interactivity, customization, and navigation. This user testing allowed
        us to:


        *   **Increase service plan options:** Cater to a wider audience with
        diverse needs.

        *   **Improve service categorization:** Make it easier for users to find
        the right service.

        *   **Streamline purchase interaction:** Reduce complexity and enhance
        user flow.

        *   **Update iconography:** Enhance visual clarity and user
        understanding.

        *   **Refine technical information:** Present assistance details in a
        clear and user-friendly way.

        *   **Reimagine product communication:** Balance credibility with
        user-friendly language.

        from user testing. This led to a significant improvement in user
        experience and, ultimately, a new project for Estúdio Marte with Ciclic
        after a noticeable 17% increase in conversions when the product was
        relaunched.
